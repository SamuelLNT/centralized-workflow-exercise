## Ingredients:

- 2 cups of fruit
- 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon honey

## Instructions:

Blend 2 cups of fruit (fresh or frozen) with Greek yogurt, 1 cup of milk, 
and 2 tablespoons of honey or maple syrup. 
Pour the smooth mixture into molds, stick sticks into them, 
and freeze for at least 4–8 hour
enjoy!
