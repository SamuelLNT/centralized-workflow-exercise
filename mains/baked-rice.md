# Baked rice recipe (u can make it to vgean if you want)
This is a portion for 2 :)

## Ingredients

- **1-3** cup of rice (1 cup refers to around 100g of rice)
- **1-2** onions, diced
- **1/2 cup** grated Parmesan cheese (V: vegan parmesan)
- **500g** of your choice of meat, cut into smaller pieces
- **8-10 cloves** of garlic, diced
- **2 cups** shredded mozzarella cheese (V: vegan mozarella)
- **1 lb** fresh spinach, rinsed and chopped (or use frozen spinach, thawed and drained)
- **1** medium zucchini, thinly sliced
- **1** bell pepper, diced
- **2 cans* of canned soup
- **1 tablespoon** olive oil
- **Salt and pepper** to taste
- **1 teaspoon** dried oregano or Italian seasoning
- **Fresh basil** for garnish (optional)


## Instructions

1. **Preheat Oven:**
   - Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. **Prepare Vegetables:**
   - Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until softened.
   - Add the bell pepper, zucchini, and spinach. Cook until the vegetables are
     just tender and the spinach has wilted. Season with salt, pepper, and
     dried oregano.
   - Remove from heat and set aside.

3. **set up the plate for baking**
   - put the rice raw on the bottom
   - pour a canned soup in
   - put the zuchinni and onions on top of the rice
   - put the meats on
   - pour another canned soup

4. **Bake:**
   - Cover the rice with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 2 hours.
   - Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

5. **Serve:**
   - Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
   - Serve warm and enjoy!
