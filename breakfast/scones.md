# Scones

## Ingredients
- 4 dl Flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 2 pinches of salt
- 50 g butter
- 2 dl milk

## Instructions
1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
2. Add butter (small parts at a time), then milk and mix to make a dogh
3. Make in to small buns and put on a oven tray
4. Bake for 10-12 minutes
5. Serve fresh with marmalade or cheese
